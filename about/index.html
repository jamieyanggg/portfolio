Core Values

What drives
my work.

✦

Empathy

I design and listen with empathy to learn how users think and feel when interacting with products to create experiences that are intuitive, reduce friction, and genuinely support user needs.

✦

Authenticity

I value authenticity in my design and creating experiences that feel honest and trustworthy, building stronger user connections and more meaningful interactions.

✦

Reliability

I am dependable and committed to delivering thoughtful, high-quality work. My reliability allows teams to trust my process and ensures projects move forward smoothly.

✦

User-Focused

I prioritize the user in every stage of the design process, grounding decisions in real needs and behaviors. This ensures the solutions I create are not only usable, but helpful and relevant.

✦

Curiosity

I approach design with curiosity and a mindset of continuous learning. I actively seek feedback and new perspectives, allowing me to grow quickly and improve the quality of my work over time.

✦

Collaboration

I value clear communication and collaborative problem-solving. I work effectively with cross-functional teams to align ideas and create cohesive, user-centered solutions.

✦

Problem Solver

By breaking down complex challenges and iterating on solutions, I create clear, effective experiences that address both user and product needs.
