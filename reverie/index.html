Reverie

A personalized mindfulness app designed to help users manage stress and build lasting self-care habits.

Mobile App UX/UI Design 2025
01 OVERVIEW

About the Project

Reverie is a mobile donation experience designed around calm, cosmic aesthetics and emotional gratification. Rather than presenting charity as obligation, Reverie reframes giving as a ritual of hope, self-forgiveness, and daily completion

02 CHALLENGE

The Challenge

Young adults (Gen Z & Millennials) are cause-driven and socially aware, yet frequently fail to donate consistently despite strong values.

How might we design a donation experience that feels emotionally rewarding, habitual, and socially meaningful — without overwhelming or alienating users?

03 PROBLEM

Problem Statement

Gen Z and Millennials want to advocate for causes and feel impactful, but existing donation platforms feel transactional, untrustworthy, or emotionally distant. They need a simple, aesthetically pleasing, emotionally resonant system that transforms small daily actions into meaningful rituals — without feeling financially burdensome or manipulative .

04 OBJECTIVES

Objectives & Research Approach

Using Design Thinking methodology, I conducted interviews and surveys to identify key user behaviors and pain points.
